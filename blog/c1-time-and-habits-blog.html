Time and Habits

which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I haven’t tried time box yet however I used the Toggle to check my time sending and using on my task. I tried to set up small goal to achieve for a task. I also do Yoga, Walking, and going to gym to refresh myself.

what did you find interesting?

Both Meditation and Pomodoro Technique were quite interesting. Meditation has lots of benefits such as Better focus, less anxiety, more creativity and compassion, more creativity, less stress Pomodoro Technique-focus on one thing for 25 minutes then take a five minute break. it’s about learning how you work so you can save time in the future.

what worked for you?

When I tried to finish the small task, it gives me confident and achievement. To achieve goals, I need focus on the small step, not the goal itself. Small steps are clearly defined actions.﻿

which will you experiment further with?

I like to Meditation and Pomodoro Technique
